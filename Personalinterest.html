Interests

I have many different hobbies and interests that mean a lot to me but if I had to narrow it down to four I would say they are futbol, movies, video games, and music.

Futbol

The 2014 World Cup was the first vivid memory I have of watching futbol. Watching the Mexico national team lose to the Netherlands was devastating but a memory I will never forget because it was the first time I had actually watched futbol. Since then I fell in love with the sport and decided to start supporting Real Madrid because my favorite Mexican player Chicharito had joined them. Since then I continue to support Real Madrid and the Mexico National team.

Movies

Another one of my interests is watching movies. I enjoy going to the movie theaters when new movies premiere because of the experience and snacks although they are overpriced. In terms of genres I have a broad desire to watch any film as long as it is well directed.

Video Games

Video games have always been a big part of my life because of my older brother who introduced them to me from a young age. I have owned different types of consoles such as an Xbox 360, PS4, PSP, and PS5. My favorite games of all time are Fifa, Call of Duty, and Rainbow Six Siege.

Music

Music is I think is so powerful because of how one can relate to it. My favorite genres include reggaeton, corridos, rap, and r&b. Some of my favorite artists currently are Peso Pluma, Fuerza Regida, Feid, and Bad Bunny.
